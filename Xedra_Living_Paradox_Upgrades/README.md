# Xedra Living Paradox - Insight Upgrades

Adds purchasable upgrades for Living Paradox chronomancers in Xedra Evolved. Access via **Paradox Upgrades** when activating your Living Paradox trait.

## Upgrades

### Sleep Acceleration (Passive)
- **Effect:** Reduces sleep time needed by 10% per level.
- **Max level:** 3
- **Costs:** 100, 200, 300 Insight (100 + 100 per level)
- **Passive** — always active once purchased

### Insight Grip
- **Effect:** Increases the duration you can gain insight from killing by 100% per level (casting a chronomancy spell grants the insight-on-kill buff; this extends how long it lasts).
- **Max level:** Your Intelligence stat
- **Costs:** 100 + 100 per current level (e.g. level 0→1 = 100, level 1→2 = 200)

## Requirements

- **Xedra Evolved** mod
- **Living Paradox** trait (chronomancer)

## Usage

1. Enable **Xedra Evolved** and **Xedra Living Paradox - Insight Upgrades**.
2. As a chronomancer, activate your Living Paradox trait.
3. Choose **Paradox Upgrades** from the menu.
4. Purchase upgrades with your accumulated insight.
